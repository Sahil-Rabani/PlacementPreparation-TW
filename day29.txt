### Aptitude (Placement Level)

Today's topics:

1. **Time & Work**

   * A can complete a job in 12 days, B in 18 days. Working together, how many days will they take?

2. **Time & Work**

   * A completes 40% of a task in 8 days. How many more days are needed to finish the remaining work at the same rate?

3. **Speed, Time & Distance**

   * A car travels 180 km in 3 hours and then 120 km in 2 hours. Find the average speed.

4. **Relative Speed**

   * Two trains travel in the same direction at 72 km/h and 54 km/h. If the faster train is 180 m long, how long will it take to completely pass the slower train?

5. **Ages**

   * Five years ago, a father's age was three times his son's age. After ten years, the father's age will be twice the son's age. Find their present ages.

### Logical Reasoning (Placement Level)

1. Number series:

   * 2, 7, 15, 26, 40, ?

2. Coding-Decoding:

   * If **MANGO → NZOH P** (each letter shifted by +1), encode **TRAIN**.

3. Blood Relation:

   * P is the sister of Q. Q is the father of R. R is the brother of S. How is P related to S?

4. Direction Sense:

   * A person walks 8 m east, 6 m north, 8 m west. How far and in which direction is the person from the starting point?

5. Analytical Puzzle:

   * Three friends—A, B, and C—like different fruits: Apple, Mango, and Orange.

     * A doesn't like Apple.
     * B likes Mango.
     * C doesn't like Orange.
       Determine who likes which fruit.
