# Aptitude (Placement Level)

## Ages

### Q1

The sum of the ages of a father and son is **60 years**.

Five years ago, the father was **five times** as old as the son.

Find their present ages.

---

### Q2

A is **6 years older** than B.

Five years ago, A's age was **twice** B's age.

Find their present ages.

---

## Time & Work

### Q3

A can finish a job in **15 days**.

B can finish the same job in **20 days**.

After working together for **4 days**, A leaves.

How many more days will B need to finish the remaining work?

---
